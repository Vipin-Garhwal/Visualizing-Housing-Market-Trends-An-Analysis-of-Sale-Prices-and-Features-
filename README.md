# Visualizing Housing Market Trends: An Analysis of Sale Prices and Features

**Project for ABC Company**

## Project Overview
This Tableau-based analysis examines comprehensive housing data to uncover insights into sale prices, renovation impact, and housing features that influence market trends.

## Key Objectives
- Understand factors influencing house sale prices
- Analyze the impact of renovations on pricing
- Visualize distribution of house features (bedrooms, bathrooms, floors)
- Inform strategic pricing and market competitiveness decisions

## Scenarios & Visualizations

### Scenario 1: Overall Data Overview (KPI Dashboard)
- Count of housing records
- Average sales price
- Total basement area in square feet

### Scenario 2: Total Sales by Years Since Renovation (Histogram)
- Distribution of sales across renovation timelines
- Price correlation with renovation recency

### Scenario 3: House Age by Renovation Status (Pie Chart)
- Proportion of renovated vs. non-renovated homes
- Age group distribution

### Scenario 4: House Age by Features (Grouped Bar Chart)
- Distribution across bathrooms, bedrooms, and floors
- Feature-based age patterns
